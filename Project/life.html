INDIA IN AGRICULTURE

  • +

    Agriculture is the most important sector of Indian Economy. Indian agriculture sector accounts for 18 per cent of India's gross domestic product (GDP) and provides employment to 50% of the countries workforce. India is the world's largest producer of pulses, rice, wheat, spices and spice products.

    Categories of Crops in India:
    Food Crops (Wheat, Maize, Rice, Millets and Pulses etc.)
    Cash Crops (Sugarcane, Tobacco, Cotton, Jute and Oilseeds etc.)
    Plantation Crops (Coffee, Coconut, Tea, and Rubber etc.)
    Horticulture crops (Fruits and Vegetables)


Agriculture continues to be the most predominant sector of the State economy, as 70 percent of the population is engaged in Agriculture and allied activities for their livelihood. The State has as an area of 1.3 Lakh sq.km with a gross cropped area of around 63 L.Ha..
